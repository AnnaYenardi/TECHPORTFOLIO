I am  

Hi I'm Anna Claudia Yenardi!

I am an architect by training. Growing up, I always thought that coding is difficult, so much that I never even tried my hands on it. However, I ended up developing a web application from scratch as my architectural thesis project. That project helped me discover my love for programming and also rekindled my childhood dream of developing games. Having not received any formal training in programming, I have been self-learning ever since, developing simple games and even founding my own web-app business based on my thesis.
